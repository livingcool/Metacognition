# Mirror — User Archetypes
*Initial Research Persona v1.0 — March 2026*

These five archetypes guide the Mirror product's user experience (UX) and feature priorities.

---

## 1. The Consequential Maker

- **Who**: Founders, Executives, Senior Managers.
- **Key Pain Point**: High-stakes decisions with asymmetric consequences.
- **Dominant Pattern**: *Certainty Surge* or *System 1 Overreach* due to time pressure.
- **Goal for Mirror**: To act as a "sanity check" before making a directional move.

---

## 2. The Growth-Oriented Professional

- **Who**: Knowledge workers (25–40), ambitious, invested in personal development.
- **Key Pain Point**: Feelings of "stuckness" or repetitive career loops.
- **Dominant Pattern**: *Status Quo Bias* or *Confirmation Bias* in feedback loops.
- **Goal for Mirror**: To identify recurring mental models that are limiting growth.

---

## 3. The Evidence-Hungry Skeptic

- **Who**: Researchers, Academics, Analysts.
- **Key Pain Point**: Generalised AI advice that lacks scientific grounding.
- **Dominant Pattern**: *Planning Fallacy* in research timelines or *Overconfidence* in data.
- **Goal for Mirror**: To see the specific research (citations) behind the AI's observations.

---

## 4. The High-Stakes Creator

- **Who**: Creative Directors, Product Leads, Designers.
- **Key Pain Point**: Navigating subjective decisions (is this "good"?) under intense pressure.
- **Dominant Pattern**: *Availability Heuristic* (over-relying on latest trends) or *Loss Aversion*.
- **Goal for Mirror**: To separate "feeling" from "thinking" in the creative process.

---

## 5. The Professional Practitioner

- **Who**: Coaches, Therapists, HR Leaders.
- **Key Pain Point**: Helping *others* see their own patterns.
- **Dominant Pattern**: *Hindsight Bias* in interpreting others' stories.
- **Goal for Mirror**: To use Mirror's reflections as a template for helping their clients or teams.
