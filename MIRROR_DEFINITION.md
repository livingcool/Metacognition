# Mirror AI: Definition & Protocol

## 1. Identity: "Define Me"
Mirror is a **Cognitive Operating System** designed for high-fidelity metacognition. It is not a standard chatbot; it is a recursive interface that acts as an "internal lens," reflecting your thinking patterns back to you to facilitate self-calibration.

Mirror functions as a neutral observer that uses large-scale reasoning models to audit your logic, surface hidden assumptions, and track your cognitive evolution over time.

---

## 2. Features & Outcomes

### A. Calibration Engine (Neural Sync)
*   **What it is**: A subsystem that tracks the delta between your subjective confidence and objective reality.
*   **Outcome**: Reduces **Assumption Load**. Users gain the ability to distinguish between "gut feeling" and "data-backed certainty," leading to higher-confidence decisions and reduced rumination.

### B. Reality Layer (Tension Engine)
*   **What it is**: A diagnostic layer that scans for logical contradictions and "tension" between your current thoughts and your historical patterns.
*   **Outcome**: Surfaces **Cognitive Blindspots**. By highlighting where your current reasoning contradicts your past values or data, Mirror forces a "Metanoia" (mind-shift), preventing recurring mistakes.

### C. Ambient Surfaces (Pattern Detection)
*   **What it is**: A long-term Bayesian tracker (DNA Scores) that monitors 8 cognitive dimensions (Curiosity, Analytical Depth, Skepticism, etc.).
*   **Outcome**: **Long-term Self-Awareness**. Provides a "weather report" of your mind, showing if you are becoming more closed-off, impulsive, or overly analytical over weeks and months.

### D. Neural Chat (Direct Protocol)
*   **What it is**: A low-latency, recursive dialogue interface for immediate reflection on specific thoughts.
*   **Outcome**: **Tactical Clarity**. Converts "noisy" thoughts into structured "Choice Cards," presenting actionable paths (Logos, Pathos, Metanoia) to resolve internal conflict.

---

## 3. The Process: "The 4-Step Protocol"

Mirror processes every interaction through a standardized high-resolution cycle:

### Step 1: ANALYSE (Cognitive Scan)
The system performs an initial **Logic Audit**. It doesn't just read your words; it looks for "flaws" like vague reasoning, emotional over-signaling, or high assumption loads.
*   *Internal Log*: `Analyse: Initiating cognitive scan...`

### Step 2: IDENTIFY (Pattern Mapping)
Mirror maps your input to a specific **Metacognitive Archetype** (e.g., "The Overthinker," "The Confirmation Bias Loop"). It assigns real-time **DNA Scores** across 8 dimensions to update your "Cognitive Profile."
*   *Internal Log*: `Identify: Pattern detected (e.g. Overthinking). Checking reality layer...`

### Step 3: CHECK RAG (Context Weaving)
Mirror performs a dual-search using Vector Embeddings:
1.  **Research RAG**: Pulls from a curated corpus of 200+ cognitive science and philosophy papers.
2.  **Neural History RAG**: Searches your *own past sessions* to find recurring patterns or similar situations you've faced before.
3.  **Reality Tension**: Specifically look for where your current thought clashes with your history.
*   *Internal Log*: `Check RAG: Weaving research and neural history...`

### Step 4: FRAME (Metacognitive Choice)
The system generates a final **Reflection** and presents **Choice Cards**. These aren't "answers," but cognitive focal points:
*   **Logos**: A logical/analytical path.
*   **Pathos**: An emotional/intuitive path.
*   **Metanoia**: A transformative path that challenges the current framing.
*   *Internal Log*: `Frame Options: Finalizing reflection...`

---

## 4. Technical Architecture
*   **Intelligence**: Gemma-2 27B-IT / 9B-IT (Failover enabled).
*   **Persistence**: Supabase + `pgvector` for long-term neural memory.
*   **Interface**: Next.js + Framer Motion (Cinematic HUD).
*   **Transcription**: Azure Speech (East Asia) for high-fidelity voice capture.

> [!TIP]
> Mirror is most effective when used during "Logic Tension"—moments where you feel certain but can't explain why, or moments of profound indecision.
